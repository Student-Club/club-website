About Us

Welcome to IGSSC

The IGS Student Club began its activities in January 2013 with a handful of members and has been steadily growing, both in number and activity. The club works around two basic tenets:

Promote interdisciplinary knowledge and skill exchange.

Build a cohesive culture amongst the IGS student body.

Our Team

All IGS students are welcome and encouraged to participate in these events to build a strong social and professional network for the duration of our PhD's and beyond.

about VIKRAM SHENOY HANDIRU

I am a 3rd year Ph.D. researcher from NITHM who is passionate about working on Brain Computer Interfaces. My research involves signal processing and machine learning algorithms to push the boundaries of the state-of-the-art in decoding the movement imagination. I graduated with a Bachelors in Technology (B.Tech) from National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal, India. Soon after my B.Tech, I decided to pursue my Ph.D. I have been involved with the IGS student club since 2014 at various positions, currently as the President.

During my leisure, I love to watch movies, TV Series, playing chess, read about the politics, technology, and sports. I love adventurous sports like skydiving, bungee jumping, skiing, scuba diving and much more. Awaiting to tick off items on my bucket list whenever I get an opportunity.
